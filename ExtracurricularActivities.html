Extracurricular Activities

1. Manitoba Cricket Association

​As a player from the Manitoba Cricket Association (MCA), I am proud to be part of a vibrant and growing cricket community in Manitoba. The MCA provides an inclusive and supportive environment for cricket enthusiasts of all ages and skill levels. Through my involvement with the MCA, I have had the opportunity to develop my cricketing skills, participate in competitive leagues and tournaments, and form lasting friendships with fellow players. The MCA organizes various programs and initiatives aimed at promoting cricket and nurturing talent in the region.

2. South Winnipeg Community Center Soccer Club

​As a player from the South Winnipeg Community Center Soccer Club, I have had an incredible experience being part of this team. The club has provided me with the opportunity to develop my soccer skills and be part of a supportive and inclusive community. The coaching staff has been exceptional, offering guidance, training, and motivation to help me improve my game. Being a member of the South Winnipeg Community Center Soccer Club has allowed me to form strong bonds with my teammates.
